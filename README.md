# Selona

**Your private serenity space.**

---

## A Moment Just for You

Everyone deserves a space that's truly their own — a quiet corner where you can unwind, relax, and enjoy your personal collection without worry.

Selona is a private media viewer designed for those moments. No judgment. No compromise. Just you, your content, and complete peace of mind.

---

## Why Selona?

### Truly Private
- **Military-grade encryption** — Your files are encrypted the moment they enter Selona
- **No cloud, no sync, no tracking** — Everything stays on your device
- **Panic mode** — One shake and it's gone (fake calculator appears instead)

### Truly Yours
- **One-handed operation** — Because convenience matters
- **Screen stays awake** — No interruptions at the wrong moment
- **Resume where you left off** — Videos remember your position
- **Slow motion & frame step** — Take your time

### Truly Universal
- **Every platform** — iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, Linux
- **Same experience everywhere** — Your private space, any device
- **No ads, no subscriptions** — Own it forever

## Getting Started

1. **Set your passphrase** — A 9-character key that only you know
2. **Import your folders** — They're encrypted automatically
3. **Delete originals** (optional) — Leave no trace
4. **Enjoy** — Your private sanctuary awaits
